About BULMUCOS

Bukoto Lwengo Multipurpose Cooperative Society is registered by the government of Uganda with REG.NO.11704/RCS to ensure authenticity of its work as approved by the regulations of the government to promote quality of our output and credibility of the services provided to the population.


Vision

To be the leading cooperative society in Lwengo district in providing agriculutural marketing services to its members.

Mission

To improve members' income through agri business training, banking and marketing produce through provision of acess to high quality inputs.

Purpose

To enhance memebrs' income through Value Addition and Marketing of their produce


Located in Kyazanga Town Council, Lwengo District, Uganda, BULMUCOS is committed to improving livelihoods through quality agricultural production, farmer empowerment, cooperative development and export-oriented business with a focuss on growth of Women and Youths to build a stronger generation of committed and resilient farmers who earn favorably from their work while ensuring eco friendly Agricultural practices.
